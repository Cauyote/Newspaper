French soldiers discover mysterious effigy after battle of Verdun

After the battle between the French and German forces, a French infantryman reported locating a totem like structure with similar engravings to the figurine found in Dunwich, England. Standing roughly nine feet tall, this statue seems to cause nearby soldiers paranoia and light headaches.

A Third Threat enters the War!

A large mass of cultists striving from the western side of France to the snowy regions of Russia. These beings don’t go by a names but soldiers seem to call them the “Cultists of No Man’s Land.” due to the fact that fighting in trenches against these beings result in heavy losses. Go to your nearest recruiting station today to save the world!

A Third Threat enters the War! (continued)

News spread as these cultists arrive in towns and turn so many innocent lives into strange and disfigured creatures no man has ever seen! The word is that these many individuals are trying to summon an “Old God” to leave ruin and destroy the world as we know it! We must band together in order to destroy the greater evil, grab whatever you can and be prepared for the great war to come!

Germans siding with cultist following!

Reports say that German forces are allying with the cultists in order to increase their power. British troops have encountered these men and say “They have no soul left in them, just a carcass out for bloodshed.” Other reports say that in order to defeat such a horrible creature, you must hit em’ where it hurts, right between the eyes! If Germany wasn’t powerful enough with their skilled infantry, they now have unholy warriors to aid them, watch out!

British forces invaded by cultist spawns

Recent reports inform us of a crazed cultist who summoned a massive wave of elder god spawns. At the same time a nearby squad of British infantry was assigned to find and kill this crazed man, sources say. Suddenly the unknown beings began to engage the British forces, utterly annihilating them.

American troops bringing new aid to the war?

A group of specialists that have prepared themselves for this type of event have joined the ranks of U.S. troops in aiding them not only against the Germans but also this cult following spreading all across Europe! They bear weapons of odd mechanisms like the “Tesla Cannon” which has the capability to shoot lightning from the conductors at the base of the rifle! This oddity is one of many weapons used to evaporate the enemy in order to protect the lands we know.
